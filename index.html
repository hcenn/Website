Our Mission

Safe Harbor exists to offer compassion, connection, and resources to anyone struggling—whether you’re an escort, a recovering addict, unhoused, impacted by domestic violence, struggling with suicidal thoughts, or just feeling forgotten by society. You deserve dignity, support, and the chance for a better tomorrow.

Escorts & Sex Workers

Nonjudgmental help, safety info, and respect.

Homeless & Unhoused

Access to essentials and paths to stability.

Recovering Addicts

Support groups, resources, and community strength.

Suicide Prevention

Compassionate crisis support for those struggling with suicidal thoughts.

Domestic Violence

Assistance, resources, and a safe path forward for survivors of domestic abuse.

What We Offer

  • Emergency needs (food, clothes, hygiene)
  • Safe space & nonjudgmental counseling
  • Suicide prevention resources & crisis support
  • Support for those experiencing domestic violence
  • Addiction recovery support
  • Guidance for legal/health services
